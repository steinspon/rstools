How To Use▶
Click to place. For line/rect/circle: click start then end. For path/polygon: click to add points, double-click or Enter to finish, Esc to cancel. Polygon closes when clicking near its first point.
Callout anchors snap to corners and edge-centers of lines/paths/polygons/rectangles, and to circle circumferences.
