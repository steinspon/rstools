# Well Architecture Plotter (WAP)

The Well Architecture Plotter helps engineers model radial and longitudinal views of a well plan.

## Typical use

1. Open the wiki home page from [RSTools Wiki](../index.md).
2. Define casing, hole sections, and key dimensions.
3. Review geometry in cross section and longitudinal views.
4. Export visuals for technical communication.

## Practical notes

- Keep units consistent across all sections.
- Validate trajectories with a second reviewer before sharing final graphics.
