# Time Converter

The **Time Converter** gives a quick side-by-side clock view and a meeting planner conversion on one page.

## What it does

- Shows live clocks in large 24-hour format cards.
- Loads **Local**, **Calgary**, and **Dubai** by default.
- Lets you add extra clocks such as Houston, Malaysia, and Perth from a common timezone list.
- Remembers your selected extra clocks between visits using browser storage/cookies.

## Meeting conversion

Use the converter section below the clocks to:

1. Pick a source location from the clocks currently shown.
2. Enter a date and 24-hour meeting time.
3. See the equivalent time in all other visible locations.

## Where to open it

- Tool page: `time-converter.html`
